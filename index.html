iOS

Notice (Important)

Due to incomplete verification on the latest version of iOS,
all apps provided by ASoft are currently temporarily suspended from distribution.

This action has been taken to prioritize safety and quality.
Distribution will be resumed sequentially once all issues have been resolved and verification has been completed.

Apps that are already installed can continue to be used.
However, please note that if an app is deleted, it cannot be reinstalled until distribution resumes.

We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and appreciate your understanding.

Please note that some apps were developed with strong dependencies on iOS specifications at the time of their original development.
As a result, it may currently be difficult to reproduce and fully verify their behavior on the latest versions of iOS.
Therefore, some apps may not be re-released in the same form.
