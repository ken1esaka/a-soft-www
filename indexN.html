iOS

Notice (Important)

All ASoft apps are temporarily suspended from distribution.

This is because testing on the latest version of iOS has not yet been fully completed, and we have decided to pause distribution to ensure safety and quality.

Apps that are already installed can continue to be used.
However, please note that once an app is deleted, it cannot be reinstalled until distribution resumes, even if it was previously purchased.

We will resume distribution as soon as verification and testing are completed.
Thank you for your understanding.

Please note that some apps were developed with strong dependencies on the iOS specifications at the time of their creation.
As a result, it is currently difficult to accurately reproduce and verify their behavior on the latest versions of iOS.
Therefore, some apps may not be available for re-release in the same form.
